2023-02-18 Pleiades

Thought I'd make another attempt at Pleiades (M45) again after long. The middle of Oslo doesn't provide the best lighting conditions, but I didn't want to waste an evening with perfect weather and no moon.

I'm stoked that I was able to capture anything at all of the reflection nebula! Only a small portion of the faint blue sheen are visible here, and I have a long way to go in capturing The Seven Sisters in all their glory, but this gets me excited about what's possible under better conditions and longer exposures.
