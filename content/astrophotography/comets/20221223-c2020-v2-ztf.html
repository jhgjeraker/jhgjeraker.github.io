2022-12-23 C/2020 V2 (ZTF)

My first ever attempt at capturing a comet, C/2020 V2 (ZTF).
  • Location
    • Name: Bergen, Norway
    • Lat Long: 60.31, 5.27
    • Datetime: December 23rd, ~21:00h
    • Bortle Scale: Class 8
  • Gear
    • Camera: Fujifilm X-T1
    • Lens: Pentax Takumar 300mm f/4
    • Tracking: None/Tripod
  • Exposure
    • Aperture: f/4.8
    • Focal Length: 300mm
    • Shutter Speed: 10/1s
    • ISO: 3200
  • Processing
    • Light Frames: 30
    • Raw Converter: RawTherapee
    • Stacker: DeepSkyStacker
    • Total Exposure: 5min 0s
    • Editor: GIMP

Result

I'm excited that the comet is visible, even if faintly. One can barely see the coma as the comet traverses "upwards" in the picture below. The trailing stars are an effect of the stacking anchors at the comet's body.

At the time of writing, 2022-12-23, the estimated magnitude is 10.96, and the comet can be found "close" to Polaris at around Ra04h18m/Dec+85Â°10' [source].
