2022-08-21 Third Quarter Moon

My first attempt photographing the moon.

  • Location
    • Name: Oslo, Norway
    • Lat Long: 59.92, 10.72
    • Datetime: August 21th, ~01:00h
    • Bortle Scale: Class 9
  • Gear
    • Camera: Fujifilm X-T1
    • Lens: Pentax SMC Takumar 300mm f/4
  • Exposure
    • Aperture: f/8
    • Focal Length: 300mm
    • Shutter Speed: 1/60s
  • Processing
    • Light Frames: 30
    • Raw Converter: RawTherapee
    • Stacker: AutoStakkert!
    • Editor: GIMP

Cropped Result

I'm amazed at how much detail this old telephoto lens managed to capture squinting at the moon. Definitely looking forward to trying this at a full moon.
