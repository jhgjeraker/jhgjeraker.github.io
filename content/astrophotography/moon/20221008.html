2022-10-08 Full Moon

Another stack of the Moon, this time just 1 day shy of a full Moon.

While a full Moon is nice, craters or other interesting structures are harder to see due to the direct sunlight, resulting in a somewhat flat image.

Conjunction

On this day we also had a Moon/Jupiter conjunction around a shared right ascension of 0h10m.
