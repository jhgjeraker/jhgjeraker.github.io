2024-09-01 Cygnus Region

First light for my Fujifilm X100VI. I didn't bring my astrophotography gear to Finse, but wide-angled lenses can do wonders too.

This is my third shot of the general Cygnus region and it is interested to see how different all of them came out. They're all shot with widely different gear/lenses, but even the colors are quite different. Variation is nice.
