2023-04-01 C14 Double Cluster

I can't believe I've waited this long before attempting to capture the Double Cluster! It's the perfect target under the poor conditions I had this day as they shine brilliantly through pretty bad light pollution.

I did have some problems with the light pollution gradient caused by the blinding bright Bergen City towards the bottom right, but I think it came out well enough. There are no nebulae in this frame, so I could be pretty heavy-handed with the histogram subtraction.
