2023-04-01 IC 1805 Heart & IC 1848 Soul Nebula

Alight, so this image is very overprocessed, but I really wanted to get something of the Heart and Soul nebulae on this day, despite the poor conditions, so I stretched until the histogram could take no more.

From the left, you can see just the faintest trace of the Soul, Heart, and Fish Head nebulae among the noise and artifacts. It's not great, but I'll use this as a benchmark for improvement going further!
