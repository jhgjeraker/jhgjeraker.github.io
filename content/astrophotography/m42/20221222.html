2022-12-22 M42 Orion Nebula

My second attempt at photographing Messier 42, this time from the light-polluted sky of Bergen city.

There are some pretty obvious color fringing on the stars, mostly caused by leaving the lens wide open, but other than that I think it came out quite well. You can even see some structure in the nebula. Pretty neat.

If the nebula is this visible in the middle of a bortle scale 8 city I'm really looking forward to capturing it under some actual dark skies.
