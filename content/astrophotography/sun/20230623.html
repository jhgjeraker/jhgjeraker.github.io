2023-06-23 Sun

Single shot of the Sun. There is, as expected, a lot of activity these days! Also experimented with various demoasicing algorithms in the raw conversion. I think this came out much sharper than last time.

There are some interesting "white patterns" northwest and sout-southwest in the image that I think are slightly too structural to be just noise. While I have yet to read up on the sun's composition in the various spectra I know that ivisible light is not the best band for looking at turbulance, but I'd imaging these patterns may be faint traces of some sort of disturbance. I might, of course, also just be seeing things.

It's more obvious when we invert the colors and crank the sliders, but at this point it is hard to say where patterns end and artifacts begin. Looks neat though!
