Moon and City Glow

What you see here is the effect of a 75% Moon at +40° degrees Azimuthal (top left glow) while shooting a target that is just above the floodlight that is Bergen City (bottom right glow). This area, Reksteren, gets Bortle Scale 4 or 5 on a moonless night, so this was just bad timing.

But, while I lost the eternal struggle for good astrophotography conditions this time we can still see the Double Cluster clearly in addition to a faint Heart and Soul Nebula, so I made a new attempt the next day!
