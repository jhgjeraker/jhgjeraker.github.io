2023-10-20 NGC 1499 California Nebula

My first attempt at capturing the California Nebula.

This came out much better than expected. I've not attempted this target since I accidently barely captured it on my first ever excursion, but I think it came out nice. While there is a clear lack of detail in the nebulosity, it's not just a red blob, and we can actually see some of the major structures in the region.
