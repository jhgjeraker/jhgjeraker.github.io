Fujifilm Film Simulation

Fujifilm provide a range of in-camera film simulations that try to replicate popular films from back in the day. Each simulation produce a unique look for when you don't care for raw-conversion and post-processing.

I've compiled a matrix of most simulations found in my X-T1 in an attempt to get a better overview over which simulation I like the most under various lighting conditions.

  • Camera: Fujifilm X-T1
  • Lens: Pentax Takumar 50mm f/1.4

My preferred simulations are CLASSIC CHROME and Pro Neg. Std.
