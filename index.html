Astrophotography

Bought my first non-phone camera in 2021, a 2nd-hand DSLR to learn about the science and technology behind photography. This quickly developed into an interest in astrophotography, realizing that even the cheapest of gear can be pushed to capture the beautiful yet challenging night sky and all of what it holds.

Go to the astrophotography section.

Photography

Though I find the technical aspects of photography more interesting than the art form, I appreciate the skill required to take a good picture, especially in low-light conditions. Now that computational photography lets anyone take perfect pictures every time, playing with "stupid" sensors and lenses is all the more fun.

Go to the photography section.
